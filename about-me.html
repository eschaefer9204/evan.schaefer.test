Written Bio:

My name is Evan. I'm of an undisclosed age and I live in Toronto. I love art and design and find myself reading about them often. So much so in fact that I have 2 giant stacks of books I've recently purchased which discuss different histories and aspects of art and design. I'm currently reading through Don Norman's The Design of Everyday Things.

Goals:

My ultimate goal is to expand upon my fashion design background and specialize in both digital and printed textile design. I want to design fabrics, not just for garments, but for everything; from the textiles used to decorate seats on buses to the higher-end use textiles sought after by industrial and product designers.
